Imaan & Akhlaq and the SDGs

At Imaan & Akhlaq, we are committed to nurturing ethically grounded, emotionally intelligent, and socially responsible individuals. Our approach aligns with the global framework of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) by directly contributing to specific targets through value-based education.

SDG 4: Quality Education

Holistic & Transformative Learning. We integrate character, faith, and life skills into education, ensuring students develop academically, morally, and socially.

Relevant Targets (4.1, 4.7)
  • Ensure learners acquire skills to promote values and ethics
  • Ensure quality education with meaningful outcomes
Our Contribution
  • Embedding honesty, responsibility, and compassion into curriculum
  • Story-based and experiential learning methods

SDG 3: Good Health & Well-being

Emotional & Mental Well-being. We support children’s inner development by strengthening emotional intelligence and resilience.

Relevant Targets (3.4)
  • Promote mental health and well-being
Our Contribution
  • Teaching emotional regulation (anger, fear, jealousy)
  • Encouraging positive thinking (husn-e-zann)
  • Building inner peace through faith-based practices

SDG 16: Peace & Justice

Ethical Leadership & Social Responsibility. We cultivate ethical behavior and justice-oriented thinking in students.

Relevant Targets (16.1, 16.7)
  • Reduce violence and promote peaceful societies
  • Ensure responsive, inclusive decision-making
Our Contribution
  • Teaching justice (Adl) and accountability (Amanah)
  • Developing conflict resolution skills
  • Promoting leadership through student roles

SDG 5: Gender Equality

Respect, Dignity & Inclusion. Our model promotes balanced participation and mutual respect across genders within an ethical framework.

Relevant Targets (5.1, 5.5)
  • End discrimination
  • Ensure full participation and leadership opportunities
Our Contribution
  • Inclusive participation through boys and girls (Naseer and Raiah roles)
  • Equal access to leadership roles
  • Promotion of dignity and ethical interaction

SDG 17: Partnerships

Scalable Educational Impact. Our model is designed for scalability and collaboration, enabling institutions collectively advance education.

Relevant Targets (17.17)
  • Encourage effective partnerships across sectors
Our Contribution
  • School-based implementation framework
  • Collaboration with educators and institutions
  • Digital platforms for scalability and coordination

Alignment with NCE Framework

Imaan & Akhlaq is aligned with the national vision for character development as outlined by the Government of Pakistan through its National Character Building Framework (Ministry of Federal Education & Professional Training).

Developmental Pathway

The national framework emphasizes age-wise development of values. We follow a highly structured pathway parallel to these guidelines:

  • Foundation Stage: Focus on manners, safety, obedience, and emotional awareness.
  • Development Stage: Focus on responsibility, cooperation, and discipline.
  • Leadership Stage: Focus on ethical decision-making, accountability, and civic responsibility.

Pedagogical & Structural Alignment

While the national framework provides the core direction, Imaan & Akhlaq offers a complete execution model:

  • Curriculum books with structured progression.
  • Story-based experiential learning and student-led engagement (House System).
  • Mentor guides for seamless classroom delivery.
  • A digital platform for monitoring and scalability.
Reference: National Character Building Framework, Ministry of Federal Education & Professional Training, Government of Pakistan.
