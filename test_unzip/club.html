    Narrative Identity

    Every student who joins the Imaan Akhlaq Club becomes a Naseer — a Protector of their school community (the Raia). They are not fighting people; they are fighting the system — the invisible force of dishonesty, apathy, injustice, and cruelty that erodes communities from within. This is their calling. This is their legacy.
